## Real-time Context for AI

Membit provides your AI agents with a direct line to the world's real-time conversations. Our **Context-as-a-Service (CaaS)** platform delivers a continuously updated, ready-to-use knowledge base sourced from global social discussions.

We empower developers and AI agent builders to overcome the limitations of static datasets, ensuring your applications are always equipped with the most current and relevant information.
