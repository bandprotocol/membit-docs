---
title: "Browser Extension"
---

Anyone can become a contributor to Membit's data pool by installing the Membit browser extension.

### How to Use the Extension

1.  **Download:** Get the extension from the Google Chrome Web Store.
2.  **Sign In:** Sign in with your X (formerly Twitter) account.
3.  **Browse:** As you browse social media platforms like X, Farcaster, and Bluesky, the extension will automatically and anonymously digest the posts you see and report them to our servers.
4.  **Earn Rewards:** Every 30 minutes (one epoch), your contributions are rewarded with Points, which can be redeemed for future benefits.
